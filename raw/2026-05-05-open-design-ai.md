## IV. Method / Loop

04 stages, iterative.

### From signals to systems.

Every stage is iterative, visual, and research-driven — composable files, not opaque prompts.

**01 Detect →** The daemon scans your `$PATH` for 12 coding agents and auto-loads 31 skills + 72 systems on boot.

**02 Discover →** Turn 1 is a question form — surface, audience, tone, scale, brand context. Locked in 30 seconds.

**03 Direct →** Pick one of 5 deterministic visual directions. Palette in OKLch, font stack, layout posture cues.

**04 Deliver →** The agent writes to disk, you preview in a sandboxed iframe, export HTML / PDF / PPTX / ZIP / Markdown.

> Skills inform everything. Files make it real.
